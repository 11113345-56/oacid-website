Our Services

OACID contributes to poverty reduction by enhancing community participation through the following service areas:

1. Business Development Services

We support cooperatives, MSMEs, and community-based organizations in both public and private sectors with:

  • Business planning and strategy
  • Entrepreneurship development
  • Organizational capacity building
  • Market linkages and value addition

2. Microfinance Support Services

We strengthen financial inclusion through:

  • Training and technical guidance for VSLA and SACCOs
  • Governance and operational support
  • Financial literacy and management
  • Mentorship and sustainability planning

3. Consultancy Services

We offer expert consultancy in:

  • Business plan development
  • Training manual creation
  • Agricultural value chain development
  • Organizational development
  • Research and impact evaluation
