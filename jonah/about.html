Who We Are

OACID is a capacity-building institution founded in 2020 by a team of professionals with over 15 years of experience in community development. It was officially registered in May 2023 as a company limited by guarantee without share capital (Reg. No: 80034190088640).

Vision

To promote rural development through innovations and community-led participation for sustainable impact.

Mission

To promote community participation in development through business development support services, rural finance support services, climate change adaptation, ICT innovations, research and development, and creation of partnerships for income generation and sustainable livelihood.

Core Values

  • Mutual Respect: Respect for clients, partners, and all who support poverty reduction.
  • Accountability: Transparent reporting to clients, members, and stakeholders.
  • Team Work: Collaborative efforts with partners and clients.
  • Professionalism: High standards of service delivery and client satisfaction.
  • God Fearing: Faith in the Almighty God to guide objectives, regardless of religious affiliation.

Objectives

  • Provide business development support to farmers, SMEs, and community organizations.
  • Promote entrepreneurship and access to microfinance and rural finance.
  • Advance climate-smart agriculture and agroforestry systems.
  • Leverage ICT for development and cost reduction.
  • Conduct research to improve effectiveness and impact.
  • Collaborate with partners to facilitate community participation and sustainable development.
