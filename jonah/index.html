About OACID

Established in 2020, OACID is a capacity-building institution focused on sustainable community development. Registered in May 2023 as a company limited by guarantee, OACID operates across Uganda with offices in Luteete, Lira, and Mbale.

Our Mission

To promote community participation in development through business support, rural finance, climate adaptation, ICT innovation, research, and partnerships for sustainable livelihoods.

Our Objectives

  • Support farmers, SMEs, and community groups with business development services.
  • Promote entrepreneurship and access to rural finance.
  • Advance climate-smart agriculture and agroforestry.
  • Leverage ICT for development and cost reduction.
  • Conduct impactful research and development.
  • Collaborate with partners for sustainable community transformation.
